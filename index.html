Application Strategy

Two viable routes

Employee Route
  • Easier application process
  • Requires U.S. Social Security certificate of coverage
  • No Spanish social security payments
  • Must carry private health insurance for family
  • Immigration office won’t verify dividend vs. salary in bank statements
Business Owner Route
  • More complex documentation
  • Requires translated contracts + company tax returns
  • Register as self-employed in Spain
  • €80/month social security year 1, ~€300/month after
  • Free Spanish healthcare for the entire family
